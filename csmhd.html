The CSMHD Code

    CSMHD is a compressible MHD code designed for the examination of compressible magnetohydrodynamic turbulence and shocks. CSMHD uses a 4th order central finite difference method and explicit time integration. CSMHD is currently primarily under development at the Zentrum f√ºr Astronomie und Astrophysik at the TU Berlin.
